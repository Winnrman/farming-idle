Game Assistance

Where do I begin?

Your inventory (middle of the screen) shows you what seeds you have. You can click on the farmland and plant the crops to begin the game.

How do I make money?

You can sell your crops at the shop. The shop button is at the top of the screen.

What's the benefit of farming more of a crop?

The more of a crop you farm, the more money you can make. You can also unlock bonuses by farming more of a crop.

Sprinklers

sprinklers allow you to turn ground tiles which do not have a water tile nearby into farmland. Note: The ground tile turned into a sprinkler tile cannot be turned into farmland.

Mastery System

mastery 1: Bonus: Reduced growth timer

mastery 2: Bonus: Increased yield

mastery 3: Bonus: Rare crop variant

Future Additions

Pests (bugs which may decrease crop quality / destroy crops entirely)

Weather (rain may increase crop yield, drought may decrease crop yield)

Seasons (certain crops will have to be farmed in certain seasons, and not at all during others)

Biomes like desert (Harsher environment, more rewarding but more challenging)
